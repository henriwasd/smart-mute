# Privacy Policy for Smart Mute Same-Site Tabs

**Last Updated: March 2026**

Thank you for using "Smart Mute Same-Site Tabs". Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines what information this extension handles and how it is used.

## 1. Data Collection
**We do not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your data.** 
Smart Mute is an open-source project designed to work entirely locally within your browser. It does not communicate with any external servers to send user data.

## 2. What Information We Access
To function correctly, the extension requires specific browser permissions:
- **`tabs` and Host Permissions (`*://*/*`)**: We access the URLs of your open tabs solely to identify if multiple tabs from the same website are playing audio simultaneously. This check happens entirely in real-time within your local device memory. None of your browsing history, URLs, or active tab information is ever logged or sent to us or any third party.
- **`storage`**: We use Chrome's local storage API to save your personal preferences (e.g., whether the extension is toggled ON/OFF, your audio rules, and your custom "Exceptions/Whitelist" of domains). This data remains locally on your machine (or is synced securely by your own Google Account if you have Chrome Sync enabled, which is controlled by Google, not us).

## 3. Analytics and Tracking
We do not use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, or any other third-party analytics software within the extension.

## 4. Open Source
The complete source code for this extension is transparent and publicly available on GitHub. You are welcome to review the code to verify these claims.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time if the functionality of the extension changes. Any changes will be reflected in this document and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the extension's practices, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
